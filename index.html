Hey, I'm Jack! I'm an audio journalist and producer working on dailies and long-form narrative projects.

More about me

Here's who I am.

I'm an audio journalist focusing on the production side of things — recording, mixing and editing podcasts. I've worked at two public media stations as an instructor teaching high school students how to produce radio and as a producer for WOSU Public Media's political podcast "Snollygoster" for three years.

Along with my work at WOSU, I'm the Managing Editor for Digital Content at The Lantern, Ohio State's student newspaper. As ME I've led The Lantern's integration between the print side of things and multimedia content, leading a staff of eight on our digital desks.

My Work

From pre- to post-production, I've got you covered. I have experience producing, editing and mixing a variety of styles, including news, long-form narrative and music recordings.

Snollygoster

WOSU Public Media's weekly Ohio politics podcast. I booked guests, wrote scripts, edited and mixed the episodes.

The Last Titan

A year long project I reported and mixed about Ohio State's largest donor Leslie Wexner. What was left for Wexner after the Jeffrey Epstein scandal?

The Third Chair

An award-winning series I reported and produced on a variety topics relating to Ohio State.

Columbus' Own

The Lantern's flagship music program. I recorded, mixed and mastered a variety of music genres.

Awards & Recognition

Society of Professional Journalists

In 2019, I was awarded as a finalist for the SPJ's Region 4 Mark of Excellence Awards for my work on The Lantern's podcast, The Third Chair.

"Let's Talk...A Pocket Rhetoric"

A podcast episode I reported and produced about first-generation students at Ohio State is featured in Andrea Lunsford's "Let's Talk...A Pocket Rhetoric" (W.W. Norton & Company) as an example of great multimedia and narrative rhetoric.
